TimeChained

TimeChained is a 2D Unity-based game, created for the Beginner's Jam Summer 2025 by a team of 3 developers.

Inspired by rogue-likes, TimeChained features a level with obstacles and enemies, that the player has to advance through to reach the final boss.

Reel Or Nothing!

Reel Or Nothing! is a couch co-op game, created as a part of the study programme at Howest University.

Fueled by the idea to make fishing competitive, Reel Or Nothing! throws up to 4 players into a race to become the best fisher of the day.

Players must navigate the arena, complete fishing mini-games and prevent others from getting all the fish to themselves.

2D Game Engine - feat. Burger Time

As a part of the study programme at Howest University, we were tasked with creating our very own game engine and prove it's efficiency by developing a game with it.

The created engine features it's own event-based collision system, sound system, custom sprite display logic, paired up with game programming patterns such as state, observer and command.

Burger Time is an arcade classic, where the player must assemble all the burgers on the level while avoiding enemies. Being revisited and revamped on a new engine, it is presented with additional functionality, like co-op and a custom scoreboard.

View both the Game Engine and Burger Time source on github.

Advanced Geometric Arcader

While studying at Howest University, I had the pleasure of discovering a new paradigm of Math, called Geometric Algebra.

Geometric Algebra is not just a new Math topic. It is a whole other way of thinking and interpreting geometry, where operations based on multiple matrices are replaced with a pair of primitives.

Advanced Geometric Arcader is a simple game where the cube-like player avoids enemies and gathers energy for missiles while rotating around moving blocks. It is fully written in C++, using a custom library made for the course, and features no vector math at all.

View Advanced Geometric Arcader source on github.
